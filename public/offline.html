Open Network Settings

Please select your device platform below:

Android devices support automated WiFi opening shortcuts. Tap below to launch:

Manual Route: Settings → Connections / Network → Wi-Fi

iOS / Apple Steps:

  • Open your home screen and open the Settings App ⚙️.
  • Tap on Wi-Fi or Cellular Data.
  • Ensure the toggle is swiped to ON.
  • Connect to your network router or verify cellular signals.

Smart TV & Remote Control:

  • Press the physical Menu (or Home) button on your TV Remote.
  • Navigate down to Settings / Control Panel.
  • Choose Network / Wifi Settings.
  • Select Set up connection and input router passcode.

Windows & MacOS Systems:

  • Check your device taskbar (bottom right on Windows) or top menubar (on Mac).
  • Click on the Wi-Fi icon 📶.
  • Toggle it OFF, wait 5 seconds, and toggle it back ON.
  • Ensure you've authenticated with portal wifi if playing in cafe/hotel networks.
